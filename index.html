UI/UX Design

UI/UX design, also known as User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design, is the process of creating digital interfaces that optimize user interaction and satisfaction. In technical terms, UI design focuses on designing the visual layout, elements, and aesthetics of a digital product, ensuring it is both visually appealing and intuitive for users to navigate. This involves crafting elements such as buttons, menus, and typography to enhance usability and accessibility.
